AboutLAWJDI

LAWJDI was founded in 1999. The Organisation was built on reaching out to Girls,Ladies and Women, who have been violated and denied the right to justice due to financial constraints and Victimization;we stand as voice for the voiceles and strength for the weak.

Simple Question

When did LAWJDI NGO first appear in Nigeria

According to Marvel Tolorunju, the first LAWJDI NGO was ever mentioned was a sister's seminar held in Anifuku to create an awareness of a platform where Girls,Ladies and Women can be heard and fought for.
